How do I get items to show in my local currency?

Hover over items to load the lowest price from the Steam Commmunity Market.

  • Ensure you are signed into steam community permanently on your browser.
  • Next go to chrome://settings/contentExceptions#cookies
  • Add [*.]steamcommunity.comand set to ALLOW

How can I change the background?

Select it in the setting tab

How can I give my support?

Donate to my bot by pressing the bit button at the top! For big donations you will be added to the Hall of Fame!

Please don't report the bot for trade scams. If you would like to have your donation back just ask on reddit with your profile url and items. If you try to scam the bot you will be banned from using the Chrome extension!
